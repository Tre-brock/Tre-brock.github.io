My Pizza Tower Review (Mon Apr 29)

Pizza Tower is one of the best games I have ever played. The music in this game is amazing, it is fast paced but does not feel life it is rushing you, and the sounds are also very good. The artwork in this game is amazing, at first it just looks like it was drawn in ms paint, but when you start playing you see that the animation is incredibly detailed and fluid. The art style is also amazing. But the best part of the game is the game play. There is so many different moves you can do in the game, but they all flow together perfectly. Almost every level does stop you from maintaining a super fast speed. If your reaction time is fast you can just keep mach running. Another part of the game is the ranks. They are from D-P (PSABCD). You can get a better rank by keeping your combo and getting secrets and the toppins. If you get all secrets all toppins and you keep you combo the whole time then you can get a P rank. Also there is a thing called lap 2. You play the level backwards a second time. You need to do this for a P rank. Recently the Devs who added a new playable character (he is also the third world boss). It is the noise. He looks simaler to the dominoes noid. The noise campaign is very different because his changed move set. Overall Pizza Tower is an amazing and repayable game that you should buy.
