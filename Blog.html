My Pizza Tower Review (Mon Apr 29)

Pizza Tower is one of the best games I have ever played. The music in this game is amazing, it is fast paced but does not feel life it is rusing you, and the sounds are also very good. The artwork in this game is amazing, at first it just looks like it was drawn in ms paint, but when you start playing you see that the animation is incredibly detailed and fluid. The artstyle is also amazing. But the best part of the game is the gameplay. There is so many diffrent moves you can do in the game, but they all flow together perfectly. Almost every level does stop you from maintaning a super fast speed. If your reaction time is fast you can just keep mach running. Another part of the game is the ranks. They are from D-P (PSABCD). You can get a better rank by keeping your combo and getting secrets and the toppins. If you gett all secrets all toppins and you keep you combo the whole time then you can get a P rank. Also there is a thing called lap 2. You play the level backwards a second time. You need to do this for a P rank. Recently the Devs who added a new playable charecter (he is also the third world boss). It is the noise. He looks simaler to the dominos noid. The noise campain is very diffrent becuse his changed moveset. Overall Pizza Tower is an amazing and replayable game that you should buy.
