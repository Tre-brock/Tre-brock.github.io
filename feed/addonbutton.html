A addon to view addons and edit preferences in xul based browsers (Tue, May 13 2024)

I love browsers like Seamonkey and Basilisk, but a problem I have is when you click the addons button, it just closes your tab and goes to about:addons. Modern Firefox lets you see your addons without leaving the current tab, and when you click the gear icon on the extention you can edit the preferences. So I made an addon to do this. When you click the extention (you might have to edit the toolbar and drag it there) it shows the about:addons page. You can disable and enable addons, remove them, or edit their preferences.
