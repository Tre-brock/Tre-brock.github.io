View the weather in Thunderbird! (Mon Jun 22 2024)

Thunderbird is an amazing tool. Being able to read my email, chat on IRC, have my calendar, etc, all in one place is great. But something I felt like was missing was being able to check the weather. So I got to making an add-on to do that. What the add-on does is it puts a button in the place width all the other "apps" inside of Thunderbird. What it does it open up a link in a tab of Thunderbird. This is where most of the development was. Basically what I did was try to get the colors to match up to TB, also to try to make it look like TB. After that was done, I put radar images that update every few minutes to show the weather (us only). Next I tried to write my own code to get the current weather but I couldn't figure it out. (I know I could have looked up a tutorial but I didn't do that). I looked for a bit and found a weather widget. After adding this to my add-on it was finished. It worked great for me. Until I went on a trip. The location was completely off for some reason. I might fix it depending on how off it is next time I try to travel. You can download the add-on now on the add-ons page of my site!
